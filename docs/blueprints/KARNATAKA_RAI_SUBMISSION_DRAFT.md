## Representation to the Karnataka Responsible AI Committee

**Re: Responsible use of AI for government order drafting — Aadesh AI (aadesh-ai.in)**

Respected Members of the Karnataka RAI Committee,

I write to submit this representation regarding an AI-assisted drafting tool I have developed for Karnataka Revenue Officers, in support of the Committee's objective of establishing responsible AI governance in Karnataka.

---

### 1. About Aadesh AI

**Aadesh AI** (aadesh-ai.in) is a SaaS tool designed to assist Karnataka Revenue Department officers in drafting government orders in **Sarakari Kannada** — the formal administrative Kannada used in revenue records and proceedings.

**How it works:**
1. An authorised revenue officer uploads a case file (PDF)
2. The AI system reads the document, asks 4–5 clarifying questions
3. An order draft is generated in the officer's style of Sarakari Kannada
4. The officer reviews, edits, and approves the draft before using it officially

**The officer retains full authority.** Aadesh AI is a drafting assistant — it does not issue, submit, or authenticate any official document.

---

### 2. Privacy and Data Protection Safeguards

In compliance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023**:

- All citizen PII (names, survey numbers, Aadhaar references) is automatically **masked** before transmission to any AI service
- Masked tokens are re-injected locally after generation
- Raw citizen data is never transmitted to third-party AI providers
- Officers provide explicit DPDP consent before uploading case files
- Data is stored on Indian servers (DigitalOcean Bangalore, Supabase Mumbai)
- Retention: 90 days for drafts, 1 year for audit logs

---

### 3. Accountability Safeguards

Aadesh AI incorporates multiple safeguards to ensure officers remain accountable:

1. **Entity Lock Screen:** Before downloading any draft, officers must check an acknowledgement confirming they have reviewed all facts and accept full responsibility. This is timestamped and cannot be bypassed.
2. **Self-Audit:** The AI self-audits each generated order against Karnataka Revenue Department standards before presenting to the officer.
3. **Human Review Mandatory:** The system design makes it impossible to use a draft without the officer's explicit review step.
4. **Audit Logs:** All orders generated are logged with user ID, timestamp, and model version.

---

### 4. Legal Liability Framework

- All officers are reminded that they bear full legal and administrative responsibility under Karnataka Revenue Acts and the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) for any order they issue
- Aadesh AI's Terms of Service explicitly state this requirement
- The acknowledgement screen creates a documented record of the officer's acceptance of responsibility
